Our Story

Hedge was born from a personal journey. As a junior in high school, I (Tanish Chauhan) struggled to understand the complexities of the stock market. The resources available were either too technical or oversimplified. That's when I teamed up with my mentor to create Hedge.

What started as a small project for a class assignment quickly evolved into a passionate mission to solve the financial literacy gap among young people. We believe that understanding how to invest and grow wealth shouldn't be limited to business majors or finance professionals.

Our Mission

At Hedge, we're on a mission to demystify stock trading and investing for everyone, especially young people. We believe financial literacy is a critical life skill that should be accessible to all, regardless of background or education.

Our Impact

Since launching Hedge, we've helped over 500 students at our high school gain confidence in understanding stock markets. Our practice trading platform has simulated over 10,000 trades, allowing users to learn without financial risk.

We've partnered with three local schools to incorporate Hedge into their economics curriculum, and we're expanding to more schools this year.
