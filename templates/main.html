Hedgex was born from a personal journey. As a junior in high school, I (Tanish Chauhan) wanted to deeply understand the stock market. The resources available were either too technical or didn't provide the tools and explanations. That's when I teamed up with my mentor to create Hedgex.

What started as a small project for a class assignment quickly evolved into a passionate mission to solve the financial literacy gap among young people. We believe that understanding how to invest and grow wealth shouldn't be limited to business majors or finance professionals.

"I created Hedgex because I wanted to give anyone the opportunity to take a hold of their financial future through the world of investing."
